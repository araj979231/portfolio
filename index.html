About Me

Passionate Builder.
Problem Solver.

Hi, I'm Abhishek Raj — a Senior PHP Laravel Developer & Full-Stack Engineer with 5+ years of experience building production-grade web platforms that real businesses rely on every day.

I've worked across a wide spectrum of industries — banking & cooperative finance, food delivery, job portals, real estate, education, solar energy, and FMCG — giving me a rare ability to understand both the technical and business sides of a product. I don't just write code; I architect solutions.

My core expertise lies in Laravel & PHP backend systems, RESTful API design, complex database modelling, payment gateway integrations, and building multi-role admin dashboards. I've led the development of platforms handling 50,000+ daily transactions, integrated open banking APIs for UK fintech products, and shipped AI-powered features using the GPT API.

When I'm not shipping code, I'm thinking about clean architecture, performance optimization, and making systems that scale gracefully under load. I believe great software is built on clarity — clear requirements, clean code, and clear communication.
